The Best Movies According to India

My top 3 movies of all time.

Paddington

A very cozy movie that made me feel like the world was a soft place when I was going through a difficult time.

Platoon

Pretty much the polar opposite of Paddington in every way, shows the fragility of one's own humanity when placed in unbearable circumstances.

The Old Guard

It's got love, action, found family, immortality, and a great soundtrack.
